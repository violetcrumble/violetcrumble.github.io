Services

HPC Hosting Services

Attuaris provides dedicated secure HPC infrastructures to meet your organizations needs. No matter how large or small the HPC infrastructure, Attuaris can build a solution to your specification.

This includes:

  • Business Continuity (BC) solutions for reliable business continuity
  • Scalable infrastructure for "high-usage" windows of operating
  • Redundant Headnode solutions

Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.

Microsoft Excel integration with Windows 2008 HPC

Microsoft Excel 2010 delivers many key enhancements which include the ability to run a spreadsheet against an HPC 2008 HPC Cluster. In conjunction with the HPC Hosting services, Attuaris also provides Microsoft Excel consulting and development to enable your existing Excel spreadsheet to run against the HPC 2008 Cluster.

For further information, please contact our sales team to discuss your specific needs.
