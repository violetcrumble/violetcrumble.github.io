About Attuaris

Attuaris provides highly specialized hosted infrastructure solutions for Actuarial and Financial modelers and will build out a custom solution based on your organizations specific modeling needs. The infrastructure will support multiple applications on a common grid platform. This cost effective approach saves on both initial hardware costs and monthly operational costs.

The infrastructure is built applying industry best practices to ensure optimum performance and reliability. Diligent operational controls are applied to ensure the latest software releases and operating system patches are installed as necessary. Attuaris works with all the major Actuarial and Financial modeling application vendors to apply their latest software releases and patches as necessary.

Our data centers are located in multi-homed, highly secure facilities which already meet the highest security standards suitable for Financial Services and Insurance Industry related data. The supporting fiber connectivity is the second highest concentration of fiber in the US and primary facility is fed by four electrical grid providers. Tier one data connections terminate in the facility.

Microsoft Hosting Provider and Volume Licensing Provider
Attuaris is a registered Microsoft Hosting provider and Volume Licensing Provider. This enables us to provide a comprehensive and cost effective hosting solution which can scale as your organization grows.
